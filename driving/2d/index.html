Got Questions?

We Can Help.

Should I choose automatic or manual?
Automatic is generally faster and easier to learn because there are no gears or clutch to worry about. A manual licence does let you drive both types, though. It really comes down to your personal goals — our instructors are happy to advise.
Do I need to pass my theory test first?
No, you can start practical driving lessons at any time. In fact, learning in the car often makes the theory questions much easier to understand, so many students find doing both together works really well.
How many lessons will I need?
The DVSA recommends around 45 hours of professional lessons on average, but everyone is different. Your instructor will monitor your progress closely and tell you honestly when they believe you are ready for your test.
Can I request a female instructor?
Yes, absolutely. We have both male and female instructors available. Just select your preference when you book your first lesson and we will match you up right away.
What areas do you cover?
We cover Andover, Tidworth, Whitchurch, and surrounding villages. We can pick you up from home, work, or college — just let us know your preferred pick-up location when booking.
