Got Questions?

We Can Help.

Should I choose automatic or manual?
Automatic is generally faster and easier because there are no gears or clutch. A manual licence does let you drive both types. It comes down to your personal goals — our instructors are happy to advise during your first lesson.
Do I need to pass my theory test first?
No. You can start practical driving lessons at any time. In fact, learning in the car often makes theory questions much easier to understand, so many students do both at the same time.
How many lessons will I need?
The DVSA recommends around 45 hours on average, but everyone is different. Your instructor will monitor your progress and tell you honestly when you are ready for your test. No pressure, no rushed lessons.
Can I request a female instructor?
Absolutely. We have both male and female instructors available. Just select your preference when you book and we will match you right away.
What areas do you cover?
We cover Andover, Tidworth, Whitchurch, and surrounding villages. We can pick you up from home, work, or college — just let us know your location when booking.
