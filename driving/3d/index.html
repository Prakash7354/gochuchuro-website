Questions? We Can Help.

Everything you need to know before starting your driving journey.

Should I choose automatic or manual?

Automatic is generally faster and easier to learn because there's no clutch or gears to worry about. However, a manual licence allows you to drive both manual and automatic cars.

Do I need to pass my theory test first?

No, you can start taking practical driving lessons before you pass your theory test. In fact, combining the two often helps you understand the theory questions much better.

How many lessons will I need?

The DVSA recommends around 45 hours of professional lessons, but everyone is different. Your instructor will assess your progress and tell you when you are test-ready.

Can I request a female instructor?

Yes, absolutely. We have a diverse team of both male and female instructors. Just let us know your preference when you book your first lesson.
