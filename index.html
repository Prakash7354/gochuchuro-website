What is the "Free Session" exactly?

It is a 30-minute chat with Prakash. No sales pressure. We just look at your business, find out where you are losing time or money, and show you how to fix it. If you want us to build it for you, great. If not, you keep the advice for free.

How does the Missed Call Text-Back work?

If someone calls your business number and you don't answer, our system instantly sends them a text saying: "Hi! Sorry we missed your call. How can we help?" It stops them from calling your competitor and keeps the conversation in your Unified Inbox.

What if I need updates to my website later?

Our coaching plans include technical support. You are never left alone. Just send us an email or a WhatsApp message, and we will handle minor text or image changes for you seamlessly.

Do I need to be good with technology?

Not at all. That is the whole point. We build everything, test it, and hand it over to you fully working. You just use one simple app on your phone to reply to messages and see your bookings.

Am I locked into a long contract?

To ensure your automated systems and local SEO have the necessary time to work properly and rank on Google, we ask for a minimum 3-month commitment. After that, it is a simple, rolling 30-day arrangement.
