How does the missed call text-back actually work?
When someone calls your business number and you do not answer, the system sends them a text within 30 seconds: "Hi! Sorry I missed your call — how can I help?" The conversation then lands in your unified inbox on your phone. The customer stays with you instead of calling your competitor.
I already have my own website and software. Can GoChuchuro work with them?
Yes. We are not here to replace what is already working for you. Plan C includes full system integrations with tools like Xero, QuickBooks, and specialist industry platforms. We layer the automation, AI tools, and review systems on top of what you already have, filling the gaps rather than starting over.

Not at all. That is the whole point. We build everything, test it, and hand it over fully working. You download one simple app onto your phone. That is it. All your messages, bookings, and leads appear in one place. If anything needs changing, you message us and we sort it.

We ask for a minimum 3-month commitment. That is because your automated systems and Google ranking need time to show real results. After 3 months, it moves to a simple rolling 30-day arrangement. You stay because it works, not because you are locked in.
