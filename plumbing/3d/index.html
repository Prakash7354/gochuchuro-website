Common Questions

FAQs

Do you charge a call-out fee?
During standard working hours, we do not charge a call-out fee. We provide a clear, upfront quote before any work begins. Out-of-hours emergency rates are always communicated clearly before we attend.
Are your engineers fully certified?
Yes. Every engineer we deploy is Gas Safe registered and carries comprehensive public liability insurance up to £5,000,000. You can verify credentials on the official Gas Safe register before they arrive.
Can I book an exact arrival time?
Absolutely. Our smart booking system lets you select a dedicated 90-minute arrival window. Unlike the vague “morning or afternoon” window other firms give, you know exactly when to expect us.
Do you provide landlord gas safety certificates?
Yes. Digital CP12 Gas Safety Certificates are issued on-site and emailed instantly to both the landlord and the managing agent. Annual renewal reminders are sent automatically.
What areas do you cover?
We cover Andover and the wider Hampshire area including Basingstoke, Winchester, Salisbury and surrounding villages. Get in touch if you are unsure whether we cover your location.
