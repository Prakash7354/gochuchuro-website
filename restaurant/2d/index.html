Our Story

Authentic Flavours
in Andover.

The Timur brings the rich, comforting tastes of authentic Asian and Nepalese flavours straight to your table. Our signature slow-cooked lamb and hand-ground spices are based on generations of family recipes, cooked with passion and care.

Every dish is cooked fresh to order in our kitchen, ensuring you get the very best quality whether you are dining in or eating at home.

  • Certified halal chicken and lamb throughout
  • Hand-ground spices, freshly prepared daily
  • Delivery within 6 miles of central Andover

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about dining with us.

Are your dishes halal and nut-free?
All our chicken and lamb are sourced from certified halal providers. We prepare nut-free dishes in a separate area, but please let our team know of any severe allergies when you book or order so we can take extra care.
Do you deliver takeaways?
Yes. We offer fast, direct delivery within a 6-mile radius of central Andover. Most orders arrive fresh to your door in approximately 45 minutes depending on demand.
Can I book for a large group or private event?
Absolutely. We accommodate groups of up to 40 guests for private dining events. Please contact us via WhatsApp or email to discuss a bespoke menu and reservation arrangement for your occasion.
Do you cater for vegetarian and vegan guests?
Yes. We have a dedicated selection of vegetarian and vegan dishes on our menu, all prepared with the same care and fresh spices as our meat dishes. Our team is happy to advise on suitable choices when you arrive.
Is there parking near the restaurant?
Yes. We are conveniently located in central Andover with easy parking available close by. Our team can give you specific directions when you call or message us ahead of your visit.
