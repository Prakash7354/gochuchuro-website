Our Story

Authentic Flavours in Andover.

The Timur brings the rich, comforting tastes of authentic Asian and Nepalese flavours straight to your table. Our signature slow-cooked lamb and hand-ground spices are based on generations of family recipes, cooked with passion and care.

Every dish is cooked fresh to order in our busy kitchen, ensuring you get the best tasting, high-quality food whether you are dining in or eating at home.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about dining with us.

Are your dishes halal and nut-free?

All our chicken and lamb are sourced from certified halal providers. We prepare nut-free dishes in a separate area, but please let our team know if you have severe allergies when ordering.

Do you deliver takeaways?

Yes, we offer fast, direct delivery within a 6-mile radius of central Andover. Most orders arrive fresh to your door in about 45 minutes.
