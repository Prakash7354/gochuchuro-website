Common Questions

FAQs

Are your dishes halal and nut-free?
All our chicken and lamb are sourced from certified halal providers. We prepare nut-free dishes in a separate area, but please let our team know of any severe allergies when you book or order so we can take extra care.
Do you deliver takeaways?
Yes. We offer fast, direct delivery within a 6-mile radius of central Andover. Most orders arrive fresh to your door in approximately 45 minutes depending on demand.
Can I book for a large group or private event?
Absolutely. We accommodate groups of up to 40 guests for private dining. Please contact us via WhatsApp or email to discuss a bespoke menu and reservation arrangement for your occasion.
Do you cater for vegetarian and vegan guests?
Yes. We have a dedicated selection of vegetarian and vegan dishes, all prepared with the same care and fresh spices as our meat dishes. Our team is happy to advise on suitable choices.
How do I place a takeaway order?
You can order takeaway through our online booking system, by WhatsApp, or by calling us directly. Collection and delivery are both available. Delivery covers a 6-mile radius from central Andover.
