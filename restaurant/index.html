A Culinary Journey

The Timur Experience.

Named after the zesty, citrus-infused pepper grown in the high altitudes of the Himalayas, The Timur brings a refined, modern approach to traditional Nepalese and Indian dining.

Every dish is meticulously constructed using locally sourced Hampshire ingredients alongside authentic spices imported directly from the Kathmandu valley.

Dining & Logistics FAQs

Clear answers to ensure your dining experience is flawless.

Do you cater to severe nut allergies?

Yes. Please notify us during the booking process. We have strict cross-contamination protocols and can alter specific dishes to guarantee safety.

What is your delivery radius?

Our direct, commission-free delivery fleet operates within a 6-mile radius of central Andover to ensure the food remains at optimal temperature.

Is there a strict dress code?

We embrace a smart-casual atmosphere. We simply ask that guests avoid sportswear or flip-flops during evening dinner service.

Can I alter the spice level of a dish?

Absolutely. Because every dish is cooked fresh to order, our chefs can customize the heat intensity to your exact preference.
