Operational FAQ Systems

Clear, honest answers to common queries regarding pricing and dispatch logistics.

Do you charge a call-out fee?

During standard working hours, we do not charge a call-out fee. We provide a clear, upfront quote before any work commences. Out-of-hours emergency rates apply.

Are your engineers fully certified?

Yes. Every engineer deployed is strictly Gas Safe registered and carries comprehensive public liability insurance up to £5,000,000.

Can I book an exact time slot?

Absolutely. Unlike traditional firms that give a vague "AM or PM" window, our smart scheduling system allows you to select a dedicated 90-minute arrival window.

Do you provide landlord certificates?

Yes, we process digital CP12 Gas Safety Certificates on-site, instantly emailing copies to both the landlord and the managing agent.
