Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about MOTs, servicing, and repairs.

Will using your garage void my new car’s warranty?
No, it will not. We use manufacturer-approved parts and follow the exact service schedules required by your car maker. Under Block Exemption law, you can service your car at any qualified garage during the warranty period without voiding it.
What happens if my car fails its MOT?
We call you immediately with a clear explanation of exactly what failed and a fixed price to repair it. We never carry out any work without your approval first. If you prefer, you can collect the car and get it repaired elsewhere, though the test fee is non-refundable.
How long does an MOT take?
A standard MOT test takes approximately 45 to 60 minutes. You are welcome to wait in our comfortable waiting area with complimentary tea and coffee, or we can call you when the car is ready for collection.
Do you offer a courtesy car while mine is being serviced?
Yes, we have a small fleet of courtesy cars available on a first-come, first-served basis. Please mention this when booking so we can reserve one for you. Subject to insurance and availability.
How do I know when my next service is due?
Our automated system sends you a service reminder text and email 30 days before your next service is due, based on your vehicle and the date of your last visit. No more forgetting.
