Common Questions

FAQs

Will using your garage void my new car’s warranty?
No, it will not. We use manufacturer-approved parts and follow the exact service schedules required by your car maker. Under Block Exemption law, you can service your car at any qualified garage during the warranty period without voiding it.
What happens if my car fails its MOT?
We call you immediately with a clear explanation of what failed and a fixed price to repair it. We never carry out any work without your approval. If you prefer, you can collect the car and get it repaired elsewhere.
How long does an MOT test take?
Approximately 45 to 60 minutes. You are welcome to wait in our comfortable area with complimentary tea and coffee, or we will text you when it is ready for collection.
Do you offer a courtesy car while mine is being serviced?
Yes, subject to availability. Please mention this when booking so we can reserve one for you.
How will I know when my next service is due?
Our automated system sends you a reminder text and email 30 days before your next service is due, based on your last visit. No more forgetting.
