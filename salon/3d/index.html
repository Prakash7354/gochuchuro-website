Frequently Asked Questions

Simple answers about booking, patch tests, and salon policies.

Do I need a patch test before colouring?

Yes. Your safety is our priority. If you are a new client, or haven't had colour with us in the last 6 months, you must pop in for a quick 48-hour patch test before your appointment.

Will my appointment run on time?

We pride ourselves on punctuality. We schedule ample time for every client so you are never rushed. If we are running slightly behind, we will always let you know immediately.
